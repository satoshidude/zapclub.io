# ZapClub Concept

## Decentralized Social Music Clubs Powered by Nostr & Lightning

### Overview

ZapClub is a decentralized social music platform built on Nostr and the Lightning Network.

Users can create music clubs, listen to synchronized music together, chat in real time, become DJs and support creators directly through Lightning payments.

Rather than focusing on algorithmic recommendations, ZapClub focuses on community-driven music discovery and shared experiences.

---

## The Problem

Modern music streaming is highly individualized.

People consume music alone through personalized playlists while genuine shared listening experiences have become rare.

Community interaction is often secondary to engagement algorithms and platform-controlled ecosystems.

---

## The Solution

ZapClub brings people together around music.

Users can create virtual clubs where everyone hears the same song at the same time. Multiple DJs can collaboratively shape the music while members interact through chat and community features.

The result is a digital music venue rather than a traditional streaming service.

---

## Core Features

### Music Clubs

Every user can create and manage music clubs.

Each club includes:

- Club profile
- Club image and description
- Live chat
- Member management
- Moderation tools
- DJ stage
- Shared listening experience

### Synchronized Playback

All members hear the same song simultaneously.

Playback is coordinated by the ZapClub Relay, which acts as a conductor responsible for:

- Playback state
- Track transitions
- DJ rotation
- Synchronization
- Club state management

### DJ Stage

Members can join the stage as DJs and contribute songs or playlists.

Tracks from multiple DJs are combined into a collaborative queue, creating a community-driven listening experience.

### Lightning Payments

Members can directly support DJs through Lightning payments and Nostr Zaps.

Payments are peer-to-peer and do not require ZapClub to act as a payment processor.

---

## Freemium Model

ZapClub operates a freemium model.

Core functionality remains free while advanced club management features are available through a Premium subscription.

### Free Membership

All users can:

- Create clubs
- Join clubs
- Listen to music
- Chat
- Become DJs
- Send and receive Lightning payments

Free club owners may:

- Operate up to 3 clubs
- Host up to 2 active DJs per club

### Premium Membership

Premium costs:

**2,100 sats per month**

Premium subscriptions are managed through Nostr and Lightning.

### Premium Features

#### Expanded DJ Stage

- Up to 5 active DJs per club

#### Paid Entry Clubs

- Charge Lightning-based admission fees
- Monetize exclusive events and communities

#### Private Clubs

- Invitation-only or approval-based membership

#### Featured Placement

- Increased visibility in club directories and discovery views

#### Club Announcements

- Send messages and event announcements directly to club members
- Promote DJ sets, listening parties and community events

#### Future Auto-Renewal

- Planned support through Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC)

---

## Technology Stack

ZapClub is built on:

- Nostr
- Lightning Network
- Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC)
- NIP-29 Groups
- NIP-57 Zaps

The platform embraces open protocols, user ownership and interoperability.

---

## Vision

ZapClub aims to become the leading decentralized platform for shared music experiences.

By combining community-driven music discovery, decentralized identity and Lightning-native payments, ZapClub creates a new category of social music clubs powered by open networks.
