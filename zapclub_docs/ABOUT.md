# About ZapClub

## Decentralized Social Music Clubs

ZapClub is a decentralized social music platform built on Nostr and the Lightning Network.

The platform enables people to create music clubs, listen to synchronized music together, discover new artists, build communities and support DJs directly through Lightning payments.

Unlike traditional streaming platforms, ZapClub focuses on shared experiences rather than algorithmic recommendations.

## Features

- **Synchronized playback** — relay conductor drives position; everyone hears the same moment.
- **DJ stage & round-robin** — up to 2 (Free) or 5 (Premium) DJs; queues interleaved track-by-track.
- **VibeMeter** — live arc-gauge showing room mood (skip ↔ banger); needle swings to the active neon segment, resets per track.
- **Zaps (NIP-57)** — pay the live DJ directly via Lightning; shows received sats.
- **Chat, avatars, moderation** — ephemeral Nostr events; relay-enforced bans.
- **Nostr login** — NIP-07 extension, NIP-46 bunker, nsec, or local key. No account required.

## Freemium Model

### Free Membership

- 1 club, 1 saved playlist
- Up to 2 active DJs per club
- Join any club, chat & listen
- Zap DJs with Lightning

### Premium Membership

Premium costs **2,100 sats per month** (Lightning NIP-57; auto-renewal via NWC — both live).

Premium features include:

- Up to 3 clubs, unlimited playlists
- Up to 5 active DJs per club
- Private / invite-only clubs
- Entry-fee clubs (Sats gate, relay-enforced)
- Featured listing in the directory

## Vision

A global network of community-owned music clubs powered by Nostr and Lightning.
